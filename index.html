DESIGN PRINCIPLE

The Principle of Unidirectional Perspective Glass
单向透视玻璃原理

1

This is the principle of a one-way perspective mirror. The glass surface of the one-way perspective mirror is coated with a thin silver or aluminum film, which does not reflect all the incident light, but allows one part of the incident light to pass through and the other part to reflect back.
单向透视镜的玻璃面上涂有很薄的银膜或铝膜，这层膜并非反射所有的入射光，而是能让一部分入射光通过，另一部分反射回去。

2

When the user's eyes are on the inside and others are on the outside, the reflected light on the outside side is stronger than the light on the other side of the eye, so the inside object can not be seen outside.
当使用者的眼睛在内侧，其他人在外侧时，外界的一侧被反射回来的光比眼睛一侧透过的光要强，因此外界看不见里面的物体。 

3

And when people look at the glass, because the light coming through from the outside is stronger than the light reflected back from the side, so the eyes can see the outside objects. If the internal and external light intensity changes, the effect will also be different.
而人眼看玻璃时，由于从外界透过来的光比该侧反射回来的光要强，所以眼睛看得清外界物体。如果将内外光强改变一下，效果也会随之不同。
